Starting with the Foundation

David on 28-09-21

Firstly, don't try to learn too much, it's far more productive to learn a bit a day and keep at it. Rome wasn't built in a day. The foundation of learning anything new is crucial. You shouldn't want to skip things, just to get to the good parts.

Secondly, learn your scales, don't try and remember them all, just learn the three basic ones; C Major, G Major and F Major. The G and F Major only have one note different than the original C Major key. They also help remembering chords too, like if you wanted to play a E Minor chord, all you would have to do is play the 3rd, 5th and 7th of the C Major scale.

Also, remember to have fun, don't be too worried about making mistakes; mistakes build a persons character, you might find some mistakes even good.
